BEST ANIMES/MANGAS ACCORDING TO GABRIELE

My top 3 Animes and Mangas of all time

MANGA

  1. BERSERK

    2. It has great story, with a large amount of different characters with an awesome background and the protagonist (Guts) is my favourite among them.

  2. CHAINSAW MAN

    3. Even though the story is a bit strangely written by the author, it has caught my attention also by watching the animation style used by MAPPA Studio in the anime.

  3. JOJO'S BIZARRE ADVENTURES

    4. With a great artstyle by the author, Hirohiko Haraki, it is well known across the whole world and it is one of the most famous Mangas and Animes

ANIME

    2. Full of great plot-twists and beautifully written story

  2. JUJUTSU KAISEN

    3. Great freshly new Anime, firstly saw it a bit late after it came out, just waiting for the new season to come out in 2025
